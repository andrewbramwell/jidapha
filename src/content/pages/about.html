About Us

Our Story

From Thailand to International Recognition

Jidapha's journey began over 30 years ago when she arrived in the United Kingdom with little experience in massage therapy. After working briefly in a spa, she made the bold decision to fully dedicate herself to mastering the art of massage, a choice that would transform her life and career.

Driven by a passion to elevate Thai massage to global standards, Jidapha immersed herself in extensive learning and practice. She expanded her expertise across multiple disciplines including beauty therapy, facial massage, and nail art, developing a holistic approach to wellness that addresses both body and soul.

Today, Jidapha is recognised as an International Massage Instructor and respected global authority on Thai massage techniques. As a Certified Massage Trainer and Assessor in the UK, she has dedicated her career to not only perfecting her craft but also to training the next generation of massage therapists and sharing her knowledge throughout the world.
